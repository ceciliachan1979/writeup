# 江城子·乙卯正月二十日夜記夢

|                  |                                                         |
|------------------|---------------------------------------------------------|
| 十年生死兩茫茫， | Your demise a decade ago set us apart,                  |
| 不思量，自難忘。 | I try not to think, but how can I forget.               |
| 千里孤墳，　　　 | With your lonely tomb settled far away,                 |
| 無處話淒涼。　　 | and my sadness has nowhere to share.                    |
| 縱使相逢應不識， | But say I see you again, can you still recognize me?    |
| 塵滿面，鬢如霜。 | With all the gray hair painted by time on my head.      |
| 夜來幽夢忽還鄉， | In my dream I am back at home,                          |
| 小軒窗，正梳妝。 | by the window, you are tying your hair.                 |
| 相顧無言，　　　 | We stare at each other but there is nothing to say,     |
| 惟有淚千行。　　 | and the tears just drip across our face.                |
| 料得年年腸斷處， | Year after year I will still live with my broken heart. |
| 明月夜，短松岡。 | By the bushes, over the moonlight.                      |
