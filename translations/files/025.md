# 江城子·乙卯正月二十日夜記夢

|         |         |
|---------|---------|
| 十年生死兩茫茫，不思量，自難忘。 | Your demise a decade ago set us apart, I try not the think, but how can I forget. |
| 千里孤墳，無處話淒涼。 | With your lonely tomb settled far away, And my sadness has nowhere to share. |
| 縱使相逢應不識，塵滿面，鬢如霜。 | But say I see you again, can you still recognize me? With all the gray hair painted by time on my head. |
| 夜來幽夢忽還鄉，小軒窗，正梳妝。 | In my dream I am back at home, by the window, you are tying your hair. |
| 相顧無言，惟有淚千行。 | We stare at each other but there is nothing to say, and the tears just drip across our face. |
| 料得年年腸斷處，明月夜，短松岡。 | Year after year I will still live with my broken heart. By the bushes, over the moonlight. |
